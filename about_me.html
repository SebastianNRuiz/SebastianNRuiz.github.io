About me

Currently a Senior at Cambridge Rindge & Latin High School, I enjoy exploring my interests in engineering and business through my personal and school projects. I can often be found tinkering with or teaching others to use the machines in the engineering room, and looking for my next venture in my free time.

I partake and invest my time in a variety of clubs and extracurriculars that the school offers including; Varsity Sailing, Robotics Club (Team Captain), and Falcon Concert & Marching Band (1st Clarinet). Outside of school, I also love working on and riding my bike, as well as projects such as FPV Drones, Ham Radio, Mechanical Keyboards, 3D Design, as well as recreational activities such as Competitive Cat 5 Road Cycling, Rock Climbing, and Hiking.

Outside of my more structured activities and projects, I love going on hikes and walks with my family and taking bike rides with my friends.
